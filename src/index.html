Rules

  • King: Moves one square in any direction.
  • Queen: Moves any number of squares diagonally, horizontally, or vertically.
  • Rook: Moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically.
  • Bishop: Moves any number of squares diagonally.
  • Knight: Moves in an "L" shape.
  • Pawn: Moves one square forward, two on first move, captures diagonally.
  • Special: Castling, En Passant, Check, Checkmate.

Available Superpowers

  • ♙ Pawn: Can leap like a Knight in L-shape
  • ♖ Rook: Can move diagonally like a Bishop
  • ♘ Knight: Can move to adjacent squares like a King
  • ♗ Bishop: Can move straight like a Rook
  • ♕ Queen: Can teleport anywhere within 3 squares
  • ♔ King: Can move up to 2 squares in any direction

Charge builds when capturing enemy pieces (5 needed to activate)

Click "Activate Superpower" when charged, then make a move or deactivate
